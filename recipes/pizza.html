Pizza

Description

Ingredients

  • 300g strong bread flour
  • 1 tsp instant yeast
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 200ml warm water
  • 100ml passata
  • 1 crushed garlic clove
  • handful fresh basil
  • 125g sliced mozzarella
  • handful grated or shaved Parmesan
  • handful halved cherry tomatoes
  • extra olive oil
  • fresh basil leaves (optional)

Steps to follow:

  1. Make the dough:
    • Mix flour, yeast, and salt.
    • Make a well, add warm water and olive oil.
    • Stir until soft, then knead 5 mins until smooth.
    • Cover and rest.
  2. Prepare sauce:
    • Mix passata, garlic, and basil. Let sit at room temperature.
  3. Roll out dough:
    • On a floured surface, roll into two 25cm rounds. Keep thin for crisp crust.
  4. Top and bake:
    • Preheat oven to 240°C (220°C fan/gas 8).
    • Spread sauce, add cheese and tomatoes, drizzle with oil.
    • Bake on a preheated baking sheet for 8–10 mins until crisp.
    • Serve with extra oil and basil.
