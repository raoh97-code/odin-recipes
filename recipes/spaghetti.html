Spaghetti

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 medium yellow onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 (28-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1½ teaspoons kosher salt, divided
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1 pound dried spaghetti
  • 2 teaspoons balsamic vinegar
  • Freshly grated Parmesan cheese (for serving)

Steps to follow:

  1. Sauté aromatics:
    • Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
    • Add onion and garlic; cook until soft, about 3–4 minutes.
  2. Cook beef:
    • Add ground beef, 1 teaspoon salt, oregano, pepper, and red pepper flakes.
    • Break up meat with a spoon and cook until browned (5–7 minutes). Drain excess fat.
  3. Simmer sauce:
    • Stir in crushed tomatoes and remaining ½ teaspoon salt.
    • Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low.
    • Simmer uncovered for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Cook pasta:
    • While sauce simmers, bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
    • Cook spaghetti 1 minute less than package instructions (about 9 minutes) for al dente.
  5. Combine:
    • Reserve 1 cup pasta water.
    • Drain spaghetti, return to pot, and add sauce, reserved pasta water, and balsamic vinegar.
    • Toss over medium heat for 1–2 minutes until sauce coats pasta.
  6. Serve:
    • Top with grated Parmesan.
