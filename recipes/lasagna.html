Lasagna

Description

Ingredients

  • 6 cups Italian Meat Sauce (or homemade tomato sauce)
  • 16 oz (1 package) lasagna noodles
  • 2 containers (15 oz each) ricotta cheese
  • 8 oz shredded mozzarella cheese (divided)
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese (divided)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp McCormick® Parsley Flakes
  • 2 tsp McCormick® PERFECT PINCH Italian Seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp McCormick® Pure Ground Black Pepper

Steps to follow:

  1. Prepare the sauce:
    • Cook or prepare 6 cups of Italian Meat Sauce (or use store-bought).
  2. Cook the noodles:
    • Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
    • Cook lasagna noodles as directed on the package.
    • Drain, rinse with cold water, and lay flat to prevent sticking.
  3. Make the filling:
    • In a large bowl, mix ricotta, 1½ cups mozzarella, ¼ cup Parmesan, eggs, parsley, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  4. Layer The Lasagna
    • Spread ½ cup sauce on the bottom of a 13x9-inch baking dish.
    • Add ¼ of the noodles, overlapping.
    • Spread ⅓ of the ricotta mixture over noodles.
    • Top with 1½ cups sauce.
    • Repeat layers twice, ending with pasta and remaining sauce.
    • Cover with foil.
  5. Bake
    • Bake for 40 minutes.
    • Remove foil, sprinkle with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan.
    • Bake 10 more minutes until bubbly and hot through.
    • Let stand 15 minutes before slicing
